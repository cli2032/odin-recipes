Pasta Salad Recipe (Courtesy of Ethan Chlebowski)

Description

Here's an amazing pasta salad recipe from Ethan Chlebowski. Despite the fact that I call it a recipe, it's much more a loose framework rather than a strict recipe - pasta salads are so versatile that you can add in, substitute, or remove many of the things in this ingredient list to your liking and it will still not only be delicious, but nutritionally well-balanced and easy to prepare. Make it as shown once or twice, then start adding your own twist on it!

  • 227g (1/2 lb) dried macaroni noodles
  • 227g (1/2 lb) chicken, cooked
  • 100g corn
  • 75g cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 30g cilantro, minced
  • 30g hatch chiles, minced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/2 avocado, diced
  • Your choice of dressing

Steps

  1. Add pasta to boiling, salted water. Overcook the pasta by 3-4 mins more than the package instructions.
  2. Drain the pasta and spread it on a baking sheet to cool
  3. Dice the cooked chicken into bite-sized pieces
  4. Broil or grill the corn, then slice the kernels off.
  5. Slice the onion, cherry tomatoes, and avocado into bite-sized pieces.
  6. Mince the cilantro and hatch chiles.
  7. Combine all the ingredients and toss until everything is well-coated in dressing.
  8. (Optional) Let the salad rest in the fridge for an hour before serving.
