Ranch Dressing Recipe (Courtesy of J. Kenji Lopez-Alt)

Description

Here's a homemade ranch dressing recipe courtesy of J. Kenji Lopez-Alt. It's a very solid recipe and well worth making rather than buying premade ranch dressing from the store. It's also nice because after you make it once or twice you can quickly get a feel for the proportions of the ingredients needed and simply eyeball things in the future.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup full-fat yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons granulated garlic
  • 1 teaspoon granulated onion
  • 1 whole garlic clove, finely minced or pressed through a garlic press
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh dill or 2 teaspoons dried dill
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh chives or 2 teaspoons dried chives
  • 1 tablespoon juice from 1 lemon or lime
  • Pinch cayenne or other hot pepper powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk it all together, let it sit for at least a couple hours in the fridge or preferably overnight!
